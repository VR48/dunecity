# Dune Legacy Android 0.2.6

Android ARM64 build based on DuneCity 1.0.522, with selectable DuneCity,
Tornie, and Dune2R modes.

## Included

- Tornie 1.0.522 campaigns and balance data
- DuneCity R/C/I, city budget, and city-credit save persistence
- Concrete production and road replacement fixes
- Correct close-range infantry rifle audio
- Complete active-mod asset loading
- HTTPS metaserver certificate support
- Landscape touch input, two-finger right click, Android Back as Escape, and
  on-screen keyboard support
- Android-local campaign and save loading
